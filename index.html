About

Welcome to "Survival in the Wild". This game is an exciting adventure that takes you to the depths of untouched forests and towering mountains, where you must survive using your skills and strategies. Prepare for the challenges of the wild nature, search for resources, build a shelter, and face wild creatures. Join us on this thrilling journey and be part of the real survival world.
